Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recovery time for breast reduction surgery?

Most patients return to work within 2-3 weeks, with full recovery taking 6-8 weeks. Each patient's recovery journey is unique.

Will the surgery be covered by insurance?

Coverage varies by provider and circumstances. We can help you determine your eligibility during consultation.

What results can I expect?

Patients typically experience immediate relief from physical symptoms and achieve their desired breast size after healing.
