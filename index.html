Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical recovery timeline?

Most patients return to work within 2-3 weeks. Light activities can be resumed after 1 week, with full recovery taking 6-8 weeks. Complete healing and final results are visible after 3-6 months.

Is breast reduction surgery covered by insurance?

Many insurance providers cover breast reduction when it's medically necessary. Our team will help determine your eligibility and assist with insurance documentation during your consultation.

What results can I expect?

You can expect reduced breast size, improved proportion, relief from physical symptoms, and better-fitting clothes. Most patients report immediate relief from neck and shoulder pain after healing.

How long do results last?

Results are typically permanent, though natural aging and weight changes can affect breast size. Maintaining a stable weight helps ensure long-lasting results.

What are the risks involved?

While breast reduction is generally safe, all surgeries carry some risks. During your consultation, we'll discuss potential risks and how we minimize them through our advanced surgical techniques.
